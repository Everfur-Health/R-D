💔 Pain / Distress Detection

EfficientNet-B4 + GradCAM detects pain-associated facial expressions. Based on DogFACS research: orbital tightening (AU43) and brow lowering (AU101) correlate with pain and negative valence states. GradCAM shows exactly which facial regions the model used.

❤️ Remote Heart Rate Detection (rPPG)

POS algorithm (Plane-Orthogonal-to-Skin) detects subtle RGB color changes caused by blood flow in the dog's face. No contact needed. Reference: Hu et al. 2024 (Frontiers in Vet Sci). Normal canine HR: 60–120 BPM.
Tips: 15-30s clip, dog face clearly visible, good lighting, dog at rest.

🦿 Gait & Lameness Analysis

YOLOv8-pose tracks 17 body keypoints across every frame to measure limb symmetry, head bob, hip drop, and movement consistency. Outputs a Composite Lameness Index (0–1) graded 0–4 per WSAVA/VCOT standards.

Best results: 10–30s video · Dog walking at steady pace · Side-on or diagonal view · Camera at dog height · Even lighting.
Metric basis: Weishaupt et al. 2010 (VCOT symmetry ratio) · Keegan 2007 (head bob) · Rhodin et al. 2017 (hip drop) · No MMPose — uses ultralytics YOLOv8-pose only.
